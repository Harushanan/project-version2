Collaborate

Partner with us on research, events, and cultural initiatives

Building Partnerships for Heritage Preservation

We believe in the power of collaboration to advance Tamil heritage preservation, research, and community engagement. Whether you're a researcher, cultural organization, academic institution, or community group, we welcome partnerships that align with our mission. Together, we can create meaningful impact and ensure Tamil heritage thrives for generations to come.

Collaboration Opportunities

Explore different ways to partner with the Tamil Information Centre

Research Partnerships

Partner with us on academic research projects utilizing our extensive collections and expertise in Tamil heritage studies.

  • Access to archival collections
  • Collaborative research projects
  • Co-authoring publications

Events & Programs

Co-organize exhibitions, cultural festivals, workshops, and community events that celebrate and educate about Tamil heritage.

  • Joint exhibitions
  • Cultural festivals
  • Educational workshops
  • Film screenings & lectures

Digitization Initiatives

Collaborate on digital preservation projects to make Tamil heritage materials accessible online and preserve them for the future.

  • Collection digitization
  • Metadata creation
  • Platform development
  • Technical sharing

Funding & Sponsorship

Support our work through funding partnerships, project sponsorships, or corporate social responsibility initiatives.

  • Project-specific funding
  • Institutional sponsorships
  • Equipment donations
  • Endowment programs

Collection Development

Help us grow our collections through donations, loans, or collaborative acquisition projects.

  • Material donations
  • Temporary exhibition loans
  • Joint acquisitions
  • Collection assessments
  • Provenance research

Capacity Building

Share expertise through training programs, professional development, and knowledge exchange initiatives.

  • Staff training programs
  • Professional workshops
  • Technical consultancy
  • Best practice sharing
  • Mentorship programs

Partnership Benefits

What you gain by collaborating with us

Global Network

Access our international network of researchers, institutions, and Tamil diaspora communities.

Unique Collections

Work with rare materials and primary sources not available elsewhere.

Expert Guidance

Benefit from our staff's deep knowledge of Tamil history and culture.

Visibility

Promote your work through our platforms and community reach.

Community Impact

Make meaningful contributions to heritage preservation and education.

Recognition

Public acknowledgment and credit for collaborative achievements.
